Junjie Huang is a Ph.D candidate at the [COMPASS Lab](https://compass.sustech.edu.cn/), Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), majoring in Computer Science and Technology under the supervision of Prof. [Fengwei Zhang](https://fengweiz.github.io/). His research interests span confidential computing, accelerator architecture and acceleration of LLM inference. He received his M.E. and B.E. in Computer Technology from Xiangtan University before joining COMPASS.
